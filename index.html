Who we’re looking for

We’re selective on purpose. We partner with a small number of truly unique clinicians — people who are at the top of their field and are empathetic, natural teachers. The second half matters as much as the first.

  • Expertise others seek out. Colleagues already come to you with the hard questions in your area.
  • A natural teacher. You make complicated things click, and people remember how you explained it.
  • Empathy for the learner. You teach to where clinicians actually are, not to show what you know.
  • Something to say. A point of view, a method, or a gap in your field you’re uniquely placed to close.
